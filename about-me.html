My history

I first began making mods for Spore in 2018, when I was making my first steps in entering the Spore community at large. My first mod was quite simple and had been done before, but it was an important experience for me regardless in learning how modding Spore worked, and what its limitations were.

I've also made adventures in Spore since 2011, but I didn't feel comfortable in publishing those adventures until I managed to for first time play Spore in English, which has since then lead me to slowly but surely translate those old adventures into English, reworking them where needed.

2021 was the year I created my first Spore ModAPI mod, a simple mod as well, where an animation is changed into multiple different ones. It wasn't much, but it was an important beginning step in my programming career as I entered an university of applied science for the first time.

I learned many important programming skills from there while also making many groundbreaking Spore mods, but that particular university wasn't fit for me in the long term. So, in 2024 I've enrolled into another UAS, studying for an engineering degree through the Internet in hopes I fare there better than last time.
