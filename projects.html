Galaxy Getaway

A student group project for the course "Pelien ekosysteemi" at Xamk University of Applied Sciences in Fall 2022. It is an endless runner type of game much like FloatyUFO, where you drive a spaceship through an infinitely running asteroid field.

It was released in Google Play Store under the Xamk Game Studios banner during December 2022, but as of now has since been removed.
